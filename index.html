Welcome to Ghazi University

Ghazi University offers a comprehensive Computer Science and Information Technology degree program. Our curriculum is designed to provide students with the skills and knowledge needed to excel in the tech industry.

About the Program

The Computer Science/IT program at Ghazi University covers a wide range of topics, including programming, software development, database management, and network security. Our faculty is dedicated to providing a high-quality education to our students.
